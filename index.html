Metroid NES Remake

Play Metroid NES Remake on itch.io

Game Description

This project is a modern reimagining of the classic Metroid (1986, NES). Experience familiar environments and enemies with updated mechanics, including a new shield system to defend against enemies. Explore the world of Zebes with an enhanced gameplay experience!

Technology Used

The game was developed using Unity and C#, with other tools and libraries specific to game mechanics, animation, and audio integration.

My Contributions

My teammate worked on player mechanics (movement and jump), while I handled all the world-building, enemy design, collectibles, audio integration, and remaining mechanics.
